# AWS Certification Business Wedge

Start with [QuizLoop.ai Product Design](./quizloop-product-design.md). That document is the product north star for the certification-prep version of QuizLoop.

## Why This Audience

QuizLoop.ai needs a first customer segment with urgent pain, clear willingness to pay, and measurable outcomes. AWS certification learners are a strong first wedge because they already pay for courses, practice exams, labs, and exam vouchers. They also have a specific goal: pass a certification.

The product should begin with:

1. AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner
2. AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate

Cloud Practitioner is the beginner entry point. Solutions Architect Associate is the higher-value next step where scenario reasoning matters more and QuizLoop's adaptive quiz loop is more differentiated.

## Positioning

QuizLoop.ai is an adaptive certification prep loop.

It turns exam guides, AWS notes, service docs, course transcripts, and learner mistakes into personalized quizzes. The learner does not need to prompt a chatbot or manually decide what to study next. QuizLoop uses saved attempts to choose weak concepts, stale concepts, and harder adjacent scenarios.

## Product Promise

For AWS learners:

- Add study notes or an official exam guide excerpt.
- QuizLoop creates a certification-specific question bank.
- Every answer updates memory.
- Missed concepts return later in a new form.
- Strong concepts move toward harder scenarios.
- Reports explain what to study before exam day.

## What We Must Avoid

- Do not use leaked exam dumps.
- Do not imitate private/copyrighted exam questions.
- Do not promise a guaranteed pass.
- Do not create shallow flashcard trivia when scenario reasoning is needed.

QuizLoop should sell mastery and confidence, not shortcuts.

## Initial Offers

- Free: one certification note, limited quiz loops.
- $9/month: unlimited certification notes, adaptive quizzes, learner reports.
- $29 one-time: AWS Cloud Practitioner prep pack.
- $49 one-time: AWS Solutions Architect Associate prep pack.
- Later: tutor/bootcamp cohort dashboard.

## Validation Plan

Recruit 10 certification learners:

- 5 preparing for AWS Cloud Practitioner.
- 5 preparing for AWS Solutions Architect Associate.

Track:

- notes imported
- quizzes completed
- repeat quiz loops per note
- weak concepts discovered
- score movement over time
- learner confidence before/after
- whether they would pay
- whether QuizLoop replaced or complemented their current practice test tool

## XPRIZE Story

The business story is stronger than a generic education app:

Students using AI chat still need to know what to ask. Certification learners often do not. QuizLoop turns their material and mistakes into a guided mastery path, then uses Gemini to generate progress reports and business evidence for tutors, bootcamps, or learners.

Gemma/Gemini intelligence creates and adapts quizzes. SQLite stores durable memory. The interface keeps the learner moving forward.
